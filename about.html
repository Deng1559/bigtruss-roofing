Our Story

From humble beginnings to Burnaby's premier roof cleaning service

Big Truss Roof Cleaning started with a simple mission: to protect Burnaby homes from the devastating effects of moss, algae, and debris buildup. Founded by Tim, a lifelong Burnaby resident who witnessed firsthand how the Pacific Northwest's unique climate challenges local homeowners.

What began as a small operation has grown into Burnaby's most trusted roof cleaning service. We've cleaned over 2,000 roofs across Metrotown, Capitol Hill, Brentwood Park, Government Road, Heights, and Sperling-Duthie areas, always with the same commitment to excellence that started it all.

Our name "Big Truss" reflects our core values: providing strong, reliable support that our customers can count on. Just like the structural trusses that support your roof, we're here to provide the foundational care your home needs to stand strong against whatever weather comes our way.
