Frequently Asked Questions

How much should roof cleaning cost in Vancouver?

Professional roof cleaning in Vancouver typically ranges from $299-$749 depending on roof size, material type, and contamination level. Factors affecting cost include moss severity, roof accessibility, and additional services like gutter cleaning.

What time of year is best for roof cleaning in Vancouver?

Late spring (April-May) and early fall (September-October) are optimal for Vancouver roof cleaning. These periods offer more stable weather between heavy rain seasons, allowing treatments to cure properly.

How often should a Vancouver house roof be cleaned?

Vancouver roofs should be professionally cleaned every 18-24 months due to our high rainfall and humidity. Properties with heavy tree coverage or north-facing slopes may require annual cleaning.

Why is roof washing so expensive?

Professional roof cleaning requires specialized equipment, safety gear, insurance, and expertise. The cost reflects proper techniques that prevent damage, eco-friendly solutions, and comprehensive warranties that protect your investment.

Can you remove moss from a roof yourself?

DIY moss removal is risky and often ineffective. Professional equipment and expertise ensure complete removal without roof damage. Our treatments include prevention applications that DIY methods cannot provide.

Is soft washing a roof worth it?

Absolutely! Soft washing safely removes contaminants without high-pressure damage. It's especially effective for delicate materials like cedar shakes and extends roof life by 3-5 years.
